Suman Chowdhury

CSIR- Senior Research Fellow at CSIR-CMERI, Durgapur

I am an integrated M.Tech-PhD student at the Academy of Scientific and Innovative Research (AcSIR) and Central Mechanical Engineering Research Institute (CSIR-CMERI), India. I am working on computer vision for autonomous vehicles at the AI & IoT lab at CSIR-CMERI under the supervision of Dr. Rajesh P. Barnwal. My research interests lie at the intersection of multi-label classification, visual reasoning, and scene understanding for autonomous driving. Furthermore, as part of my B.Tech project at Jalpaiguri Government Engineering College and during my internship at IIT Kharagpur, I briefly worked on distributed storage systems and coding theory.
